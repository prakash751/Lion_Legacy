BECAUSE LION IS THE KING

The lion is a wild animal. And are the most ferocious animal in the world. He is also known as the king of the forest or Jungle. As he is the best hunter in the forest and having a big size, powerful appearance. His biological name is Panthera leo.

BECAUSE OF BODY APPEARANCE AND ABILITIES

He has a strong body having four legs, a large head and two eyes which gives a glowing effect to his look. A cub has dark spots on his body which generally fades away with time. It has sharp teeth and claws help in eating his prey and also while hunting. His body is covered with greyish small and smooth hair. And the very famous part of their body is the mane of lion which means long hairs around the neck of the adult lion. The average weight of a male lion is 190 kg and the average weight of a female lion or lioness is 130 kg.

BECAUSE OF BEHAVIOUR AND LIFE SPAN

He eats flesh and has the ability to run very fast. And the roar of the lion makes him a lot famous, as he roars very loudly, as the roar of a lion can be heard from almost 5 miles distance. The average life span of a lion in the forest is 10 to 14 years but the lions in the zoo live almost 20 years as they get their food without hunting.
